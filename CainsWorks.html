Cains Works

This is my experience working with Cains Works and some of the things that I have created so far

What is Cains Works?

Cains Works is a startup video game company based out of Minneapolis, MN that is currently working on their first game. Their first game is going to be called The Star Rover, and they are hoping to finish a solid demo for the game in the Summer of 2016.

What I Do at Cains Works

As a founding member of Cains Works, I help make major decisions for our first game that we are developing. My primary role is to develop media to be used in the game and to market it.
Some of the things I do as the Media Producer:

  • Video Game Trailers in After Effects and Premiere Pro
  • Interface Art in Photoshop
  • Merchandise Art plus Company and Game Logos
  • Social Media and Marketing Assistance
